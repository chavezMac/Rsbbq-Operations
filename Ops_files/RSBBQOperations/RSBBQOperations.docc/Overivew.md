# RSBBQ Operations

Native iOS app for multi-store operations.

## Overview

This app provides:

- **Store dashboard** — Grid of store tiles; tap a store to open its detail.
- **Out of Stock** — Hourly stock status heatmap by date (PST).
- **Daily Sales** — Date range, swipeable Catering vs. Non-Catering charts and summary tables.
